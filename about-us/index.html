About Us

Fewzz is a GIS software and service company. We provide GIS infrastructure design, development, implementations and consultancy. We help customers collect, manage, and publish spatial data over the internet efficiently and effortlessly. Our in-house developed GIS viewer management platform makes our GIS implementations faster, more sustainable, and with less coding effort. We build GIS solutions based on open source technologies, so we deliver high quality products at lower cost.
